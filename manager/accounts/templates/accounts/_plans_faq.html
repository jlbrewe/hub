FAQ

Personal vs Organization plans

Plans can be added to either your personal account, or to an Organization. Organizations allow you to create teams, and invite users with different permissions.

You can create as many Organizations as you need, but each one will require its own subscription plan.

What happens when I reach a plan’s limit?

You will never be charged an overage fee. If you reach a storage limit, you will be unable to add more content until you free up some space. For compute minutes, you—or people attempting to run your project—will be unable to launch a compute session until the next billing cycle.
